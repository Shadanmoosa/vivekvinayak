I’m Vivek, a passionate photographer who believes every moment deserves to be captured beautifully. My journey began with the love of freezing emotions and stories through my lens, which soon turned into a profession that lets me connect with people and their most memorable days

Specializing in wedding and event photography, I focus on capturing real emotions, raw expressions, and the energy that fills every celebration. Along with that, I also take on product, moto, and creative shoots, giving each frame a touch of originality and detail that makes it stand out.

Equipped with my Sony A7R V and strong editing skills, I make sure every image and video reflects perfection — from vibrant colors to cinematic tones. Whether it’s a grand wedding or a small personal event, I aim to deliver visuals that tell stories you’ll cherish forever.
